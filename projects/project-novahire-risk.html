NovaHire — AI Risk Assessment & Board Review — NovaAI Labs

Board-level AI risk assessment and governance review of a high-risk recruitment NLP system under EU AI Act Annex III — 7-risk register, confirmed bias findings escalated to the Board Risk & Audit Committee, and a 90-day remediation plan

Company: NovaAI Labs
System: NovaHire v3.1 (NVA-AI-003)
Frameworks: EU AI Act Annex III, NIST AI RMF, GDPR Art. 22
Scale: 47 enterprise customers • 180,000+ candidates

Project Overview

NovaHire (NVA-AI-003) is NovaAI Labs’ internally developed transformer-based recruitment NLP system, deployed by NovaAI’s HR function and licensed to 47 enterprise customers across the European Union. The system ingests CVs, job descriptions, and LinkedIn data to produce ranked shortlists with fit scores (0–100). By April 2026, the system had processed approximately 180,000 job applicants, placing it squarely within the EU AI Act's most consequential obligations.

This project delivered an end-to-end AI risk assessment and governance review of NovaHire — classifying it as a high-risk AI system under EU AI Act Annex III, Category 4(a) (employment and recruitment decisions); documenting confirmed bias findings from a February 2026 audit (18% gender disparity, 22% age disparity); scoring AI-specific risks under a 5×5 methodology aligned to NIST AI RMF; and escalating findings to the Board Risk & Audit Committee with recommended remediation actions, a deployment decision, and customer notification strategy.

Project Highlights
