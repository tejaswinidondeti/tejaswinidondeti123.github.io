About Me

GRC professional with deep expertise in AI governance and information security risk management. Specialising in building governance programs for high-risk AI systems under the EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, and ISO 42001 — including end-to-end AI Management Systems (AIMS) for healthcare diagnostics and financial risk scoring, and board-level AI risk assessments for recruitment NLP systems classified High-Risk under EU AI Act Annex III. Proven ability to translate complex regulatory requirements into actionable governance programs across healthcare, fintech, and SaaS industries.
